003 : Hospital Lobby Animatronic

Built for the 2022 Technology Student Association (TSA) animatronics high school event, which was built to entertain children during their wait before a hospital visit. As even regular checkups and the general hospital environment can be anxiety-inducing for children, the animatronic created was meant to ease this tention by showing a monster, which is typically depicted as scary, going to the doctor to get better after becoming sick.
