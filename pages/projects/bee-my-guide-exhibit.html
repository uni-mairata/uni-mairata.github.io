002 : Bee My Guide - Electronic ARTrium

The second show "Bee My Guide" developed by the Electronic ARTrium Vertically Integration Project team at Georgia Tech is ideated by Dr. Weitnauer and created by the entire interdisciplinary team. The team, which has of all levels of students from undergraduate sophomores to PhD students each semester, is split into 4 different subteams: Electromechanical (EM), Sensor Processing and Networking (SPN), Visual Art and Sound Design (VASD), and Web and Social Media Design (WSMD).
